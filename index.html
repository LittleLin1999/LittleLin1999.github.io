Constraining DLA halo mass by QSO (DLA) x CMB Lensing cross-correlation

Xiaojing L., Zheng C., et al

We study the cross correlation of Damped Lyman Alpha systems (DLAs) and their background quasars, using the most updated DLA catalog and the Planck 2018 CMB lensing convergence field, and give a constraint on the upper limit of DLA halo mass.

Advised by Zheng Cai, Department of Astronomy, Tsinghua University.

Phase-phase correlation study on single pulses of 10 pulsars from Parkes

We study the phase-phase correlation patterns of pulsar single pulses observed by Parkes Observatory, and find interesting correlation behaviors. We demonstrate the potential of this method to further explore the physics of pulsars through single-pulse observation.

Advised by Kejia Lee and Renxin Xu, DoA & KIAA, PKU
