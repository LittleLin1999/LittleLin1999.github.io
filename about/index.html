Hi! This is Xiaojing Lin , an undergraduate student from Department of Astronomy at Peking University.

I was born in Ningde, Fujian, a beautiful seashore city located in Southern China. I finished my high school at Xiamen Foreign Language School, and was admitted to Peking University in 2017, at my age of 17. I will go to Tsinghua University for graduate education in 2021.

I am now generally interested in dark matter, weak lensing and large scale structure of the Universe. I am also attracted by statistical tools and big data analysis in astronomy, especially those applied in large surveys.
